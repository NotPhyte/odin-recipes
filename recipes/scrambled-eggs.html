Scrambled Eggs

Description

The secret to making scrambled eggs is all about patience... and the proper pan. Here's a quick step-by-step guide to show you how to make scrambled eggs, plus we'll answer your most frequently asked questions.

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 eggs
  • 4 to 6 tablespoons of milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Break eggs on a flat surface and put in bowl
  2. Add milk to make creamier, up to 1 tablespoon per egg
  3. Heat pan with butter or oil
  4. When the butter starts to bubble, or a drop of water added to the pan sizzles, slowly pour in the egg mixture. Immediately reduce the heat to medium-low and as soon as the eggs begin to "set" or form a slightly solid base (a minute or two), gently stir with a rubber spatula.
  5. Add minced herbs and other optional ingredients when egg begins to curd
  6. Remove the pan from heat and continue to stir until the eggs finish cooking, serve immediately on plates
